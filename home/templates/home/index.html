Welcome

Who I am

I'm Darren, a fully qualified personal trainer and gym instructor for the last 5 years! I completed the Image Fitness Training course and currently work as a gym instructer and class co-ordinator at Sheldon Park Leisure Centre. I specialize in spin classes, strength and conditioning and 1 to 1 personal training.

My Journey

Back in 2011 I decided to get working on my personal fitness and began losing weight by working out regularly and eating healthy, I tried everything I could to improve my fitness and weightloss! because of this I come as a veteran in the industry with a wealth of knowledge of do's and dont's and a heap of tips and tricks for your journey! I started at a weight of 110kg and now currently weigh 80kg

How I did it

Hard work, hard work and hard work! there is no easy way to get fit or lose weight it is all about consistancy! I was consistant when going to the gym, I had a plan on what I would be doing each day and I would have a plan for my meals. With that I was consistantly in the gym on the days I was suppose to and continued working hard.

My promise
